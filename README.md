## 📌 What is AudioSwitcher?

AudioSwitcher is a small app for Windows 11 that lives in your system tray. It lets you switch between your audio output devices—like speakers, headphones, or Bluetooth devices—using a simple keyboard shortcut. You don’t need to go deep into settings or click multiple times.  

The app runs quietly in the background and works as soon as you start Windows. It makes managing sound devices easier if you often switch audio outputs during work, entertainment, or meetings.

## 🧰 Troubleshooting  

**AudioSwitcher icon does not appear:**  
- Check that the app is running by pressing **Ctrl + Shift + Esc** to open Task Manager and look for AudioSwitcher.  
- Restart your computer and run the app again.

**Hotkey does not work:**  
- The hotkey might conflict with another program. Try using the settings menu to assign a different key combination.  
- Make sure your keyboard is working properly.

**Sound does not change:**  
- Verify that your audio devices are connected and set up correctly in Windows sound settings.  
- Try restarting AudioSwitcher or your computer.
